“It's been quite a rollercoaster with two fusions in the same number of years,” BARBARR added. “All the love to KOI and the supporters, wishing you nothing but the best.”

BARBARR originally joined KOI at the beginning of the partnership era in 2023. Prior to KOI, he served as the head coach for Guild Esports Guild Esports Inactive , who most notably finished in top-eight at Masters Copenhagen.

KOI spent the 2023 EMEA League season towards the bottom of the standings, ending in ninth place with a 2-7 record. In the Last Chance Qualifier, they came just one placement away from qualification to Champions 2023, but were ultimately denied in the lower final by Natus Vincere Natus Vincere Europe Rank #18 ANGE1 Kyrylo Karasov Shao Andrey Kiprsky .
